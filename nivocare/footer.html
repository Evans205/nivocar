NIVOCARE Research and Analytic Solutions

NIVOCARE Research and Analytic Solutions was founded by Eric Jeremiah, who serves as the CEO. We specialize in data analytics, programming, and research, delivering high-quality services to solve complex problems.

Our expertise includes data insights, digital tool development, and research solutions tailored to your needs. Based in Makueni-Wote Town, we recruit and train enumerators and research assistants to ensure efficient data collection.

Our team comprises data science experts, IT specialists, and research professionals, working together to provide cutting-edge solutions. Whether you need data-driven decision-making support or innovative digital tools, NIVOCARE is your trusted partner for research and analytics.
